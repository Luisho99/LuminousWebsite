Dear Reader,

We are Luminous and we would like to invite you to the world of self expression. We like to tell you a bit more about us and how we founded the company. Welcome to our store, your number one source for all things regarding jackets. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of designer jackets, with a focus on uniqueness, love and up to date for the latest trends. Founded in 2020 by a group of students, Luminous has come a long way from its beginnings in a university.
